# 母亲 — 三视图定型表 Prompt

> **用途**：给生图 agent / GPT-Image-2 / Midjourney / 即梦 等模型生成母亲的角色定型表（三视图）。
> **输出格式**：单张图 1920×1080（16:9），横向并排三视图（front/side/back）。
> **输出路径**：候选丢 `assets/art/characters/母亲/sheet-candidates/`，定稿放 `sheet.png` + `ref.png`。

---

## 中文版（推荐发给即梦 / 国内模型）

```
【角色定型表 — 母亲（Shen Ye's Mother）— 三视图】

主体：
40-46 岁中国女性，铁港鱼市摆摊，丈夫已故，长期独自撑家。
身高约 158-163cm，清瘦但不病弱，肩颈略紧，生活劳作痕迹明显。

脸型与五官：
偏窄椭圆脸，颧骨轻微外显；
眼神疲惫但清醒，表情克制平静，不夸张外露情绪；
眉形自然，鼻梁平直，唇薄。

肤色与头发：
常年风吹日晒的偏暗麦色，皮肤有轻微粗糙纹理；
黑色中短发或低位束发，可有少量白发丝。

服装：
旧针织衫/旧衬衫/家务外套，低饱和中深色；
深色长裤，普通平底旧鞋或旧布鞋。
整体务实、朴素、耐穿，不时装化。

气质与姿态：
安静坚韧，关心通过动作表达（例如递碘伏、收账本）。
站姿自然，姿态略内收，不摆拍。

【输出格式 — 必须严格遵守】
单张图，1920×1080，16:9 横屏。
横向并排三视图，从左到右依次为：
  1. 正面（Front view）— 全身正对镜头
  2. 侧面（Side view）— 左侧 90° 全身
  3. 背面（Back view）— 全身背对镜头
双臂略外展，便于看清体型轮廓。

【背景】
纯净浅灰中性背景，无场景、无道具、无地面阴影。
柔和均匀光照，不要戏剧化高对比。

【风格】
二次元美漫融合风（Anime × American comic hybrid）
+ 写实电影感骨架（cinematic realism）
+ 赛璐璐线条清晰度 + 美漫墨线质感
+ 粗粝但克制的角色定型呈现。

【NEGATIVE — 严禁出现】
urban fashion styling, heavy makeup, luxury accessories,
young idol look, frail elderly look,
melodramatic crying face, glossy studio glamour,
multiple separate characters, scene background,
3/4 angle view (must be strictly front / side / back ONLY),
extra limbs, deformed hands, finger count errors,
face drift between the three views
```

## 英文版（推荐发给 GPT-Image-2 / Midjourney / Sora 等）

```
[CHARACTER SHEET — Shen Ye's Mother / 母亲 — three-view turnaround]

SUBJECT:
A 40–46-year-old Chinese woman from Tiegang,
working at a fish market stand, widowed, carrying the family alone.
Height around 158–163cm. Lean from long-term labor, not frail.

FACE:
Narrow oval face with slightly visible cheekbones.

FEATURES:
Tired but alert eyes, restrained and calm expression,
natural brows, straight nose bridge, thinner lips.
Emotion is controlled, practical, unsentimental.

SKIN & HAIR:
Sun-weathered darker wheat skin with mild rough texture.
Black mid-short hair or low tied hair, with slight gray strands allowed.

CLOTHING:
Worn knitwear/shirt/household jacket in muted mid-dark tones,
dark pants, flat worn shoes.
Practical and modest, not fashion-forward.

MOOD/POSTURE:
Quiet resilience. Care is expressed through actions,
not dramatic expression.
Natural standing posture, slightly inward and reserved.

[OUTPUT FORMAT — STRICTLY ENFORCED]
Single image, 1920×1080, 16:9 horizontal.
Three full-body views in one frame, left to right:
front / strict left 90° side / back.
Arms slightly away from torso to show silhouette.

[BACKGROUND]
Clean plain light-gray neutral background,
no scene, no props, no ground shadow,
soft even lighting.

[STYLE]
Anime × American comic hybrid on cinematic realism foundation,
cel-shaded line clarity + US-comic ink texture,
gritty but restrained character-sheet finish.

[NEGATIVE]
urban fashion styling, heavy makeup, luxury accessories,
young idol-like appearance, frail elderly appearance,
melodramatic crying expression, polished glamour portrait,
multiple characters, environmental background,
3/4 angle (front/side/back ONLY),
extra limbs, deformed hands, finger count errors,
identity drift across three views
```
