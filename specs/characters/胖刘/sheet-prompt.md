[CHARACTER SHEET — Pang Liu / 胖刘 — three-view turnaround]

SUBJECT:
A Chinese male dock foreman from Tiegang, age 40–45, around 182cm.
Heavy, dense, labor-built strength from years of cargo handling and

He carries authority of a frontline boss.

FACE:
Broad square face, heavy cheek structure, strong jaw muscles.

FEATURES:
Thick brows, commanding and evaluating eyes, wider bridge nose,
fuller lips. Default expression: unsmiling, stern, practical.

SKIN:
Dark tanned skin from long-term harbor sun and sea wind exposure.

HAIR:
Short practical cut, slightly receding hairline, black with subtle gray.

CLOTHING:
Worn dark work tank or short-sleeve utility shirt, cheap dark durable
work trousers, old practical work shoes.

DISTINCTIVE MARKS:
Old abrasions on forearms and hands, rough callused fingers, pronounced
knuckles, ungroomed nails. Hands of a long-term manual labor manager.

MOOD/POSTURE:
Silent pressure, command presence, grounded labor foreman stance.
Not a model pose, not heroic stylization.

[OUTPUT FORMAT — STRICTLY ENFORCED]
Single image, 1920×1080, 16:9 horizontal.
Three full-body views arranged left to right:
  1) Front view
  2) Left side view at strict 90°
  3) Back view
Pose: natural standing, arms slightly away from torso for silhouette clarity.

[BACKGROUND]
Clean plain light gray neutral background. No scene, no props, no text,
no logos, no ground shadow. Soft even neutral lighting only.

[STYLE]
Anime × American comic hybrid (二次元美漫融合风), with cinematic realism
foundation, clean cel-shaded readability + US-comic ink texture,
gritty but neutral character-sheet finish.

[NEGATIVE]
gym muscle, idol styling, smiling face, polished glamour,
clean ironed clothing, model-like beauty, extra characters,
any environment background, dramatic shadows, 3/4 view,
deformed hands, finger count errors, face drift across three views.
