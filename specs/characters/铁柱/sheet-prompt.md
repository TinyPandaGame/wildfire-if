# 铁柱 — 三视图定型表 Prompt

> **用途**：给生图 agent / GPT-Image-2 / Midjourney / 即梦 等模型生成铁柱的角色定型表（三视图）。
> **输出格式**：单张图 1920×1080（16:9），横向并排三视图（front/side/back）。
> **输出路径**：候选丢 `assets/art/characters/铁柱/sheet-candidates/`，定稿放 `sheet.png` + `ref.png`。
>
> Agent 任选中文版或英文版（信息等价）发给模型即可。

---

## 中文版（推荐发给即梦 / 国内模型）

```
【角色定型表 — 铁柱（Tie Zhu）— 三视图】

主体：
30-35 岁中国码头工人，身高约 195cm。
重型高大对抗体格，宽肩厚胸，前臂和手掌显著粗大，
力量感来自长期码头体力劳动（不是健身房雕刻肌，不是健美摆拍体型）。
铁港地下篮球场“狗笼”的压迫型老手。

脸型：
宽方脸，颧弓与下颌线厚重。

五官：
粗眉，眼窝略深，鼻梁偏粗直，嘴唇较厚。
表情基线：轻蔑、凶悍、压迫感强，常有“居高临下”目光。

肤色：
长期风吹日晒的深麦色 / 古铜偏暗肤色。

发型：
黑色短寸或贴头短发，实用、不修饰。

服装：
深色旧背心或无袖工装上衣（可有汗渍、磨损），
深色工装长裤或旧运动短裤，
耐磨旧球鞋或厚底旧鞋，低调配色。

标志性细节：
前臂粗壮，手背与指节有老茧和旧伤；
颈肩和手肘附近有长期对抗磨痕；
站姿常见重心压前、胸口前顶、手臂略外张，占空间感强。

气质：
像“一堵墙”，稳、沉、狠。
动作不花哨，强调身体接触与压迫。

【输出格式 — 必须严格遵守】
单张图，1920×1080，16:9 横屏。
横向并排三视图，从左到右依次为：
  1. 正面（Front view）— 全身正对镜头
  2. 侧面（Side view）— 左侧 90° 全身
  3. 背面（Back view）— 全身背对镜头
姿势：自然站立，双臂略微外展（不要垂直贴身），便于看清体型轮廓。

【背景】
纯净浅灰中性背景，无场景、无道具、无地面阴影。
柔和均匀光照，不要戏剧化强对比。
（用于后续 shot 参考，必须中性可复用）

【风格】
二次元美漫融合风（Anime × American comic hybrid）
+ 写实电影感骨架（cinematic realism）
+ 赛璐璐线条清晰度 + 美漫墨线质感
+ 粗粝但克制的角色定型呈现。

【NEGATIVE — 严禁出现】
bodybuilder posing, gym sculpted abs, idol styling,
cartoon exaggeration, polished fashion look,
clean brand-new clothing, gold-chain gangster styling,
multiple separate characters,
dramatic shadows, any scene background, environment,
3/4 angle view (must be strictly front / side / back ONLY),
extra characters, deformed hands, finger count errors,
face drift between the three views (same identifiable person across all 3)
```

## 英文版（推荐发给 GPT-Image-2 / Midjourney / Sora 等）

```
[CHARACTER SHEET — Tie Zhu / 铁柱 — three-view turnaround]

SUBJECT:
A 30–35-year-old Chinese dock worker from Tiegang,
approximately 195cm tall. Heavy, imposing contact-build body:
very broad shoulders, thick chest, large forearms and hands.
His strength comes from years of manual harbor labor
(NOT gym-sculpted bodybuilding, NOT fitness-model posing).
He is a dominant veteran in the underground court “Dog Cage”.

FACE:
Wide square face, heavy jawline and cheek structure.

FEATURES:
Thick eyebrows, slightly deep-set eyes, broad straight nose,
thicker lips. Default expression is contemptuous, tough,
and physically intimidating.

SKIN:
Dark tanned / sun-beaten bronze skin tone from long outdoor labor.

HAIR:
Short black buzz cut or close-cropped practical hair,
unstyled, functional.

CLOTHING:
Dark worn tank top or sleeveless workwear,
dark work pants or old athletic shorts,
worn durable basketball/work shoes in muted colors.

DISTINCTIVE MARKS:
Calloused knuckles and hands, old scars on forearms,
wear marks around neck/shoulders/elbows from repeated physical contact.
Posture often occupies space: chest forward, weight forward,
arms slightly open.

MOOD/POSTURE:
“Human wall” presence — steady, heavy, ruthless.
No flashy movement; emphasize raw physical pressure.

[OUTPUT FORMAT — STRICTLY ENFORCED]
Single image, 1920×1080, 16:9 horizontal aspect ratio.
Three views arranged side by side, left to right:
  1. Front view — full body facing camera
  2. Side view — full body left profile at 90°
  3. Back view — full body facing away
Pose: natural standing, arms slightly extended outward
(NOT pressed to torso) to clearly show silhouette.

[BACKGROUND]
Clean plain light-gray neutral background.
No scene, no props, no ground shadow.
Soft even lighting, no dramatic contrast.

[STYLE]
Anime × American comic hybrid, built on cinematic realism,
with cel-shaded line clarity + US-comic ink texture,
gritty but controlled character-sheet finish.

[NEGATIVE]
bodybuilder posing, gym sculpted abs, idol styling,
cartoon exaggeration, polished fashion styling,
clean brand-new clothing, luxury gangster accessories,
multiple separate characters,
dramatic shadows, any scene background, any environment,
3/4 angle view (must be STRICTLY front / side / back),
extra characters, deformed hands, finger count errors,
face drift between three views (must be the same person across all 3)
```

## 操作流程

1. 使用“最终用版”先跑 3–5 次（同 prompt 不同 seed）
2. 候选图保存到 `assets/art/characters/铁柱/sheet-candidates/`
   命名：`YYYY-MM-DD-attempt-NN.png`
3. 提交后等待审核，选定后复制为：
   - `assets/art/characters/铁柱/sheet.png`
   - `assets/art/characters/铁柱/ref.png`
4. 后续含铁柱镜头（尤其 shot-07）统一引用 `ref.png`
